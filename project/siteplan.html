Site plan for Brazinfo Inc. Commute

Site Name: Brazinfo Inc. Commute

This site name was selected because of the content being related to providing bus lines information for commuting to the company they work in.

Site Purpose:

The site represents the official commute bus lines to Brazinfo Inc. Company employees. It allows them to check on the current bus lines scheduled times, real time information about the bus location. The site will allow the employees to suggest new lines or course alterations by filling a petition form. There's also a authenticated administration area strict for making changes on the available lines.

Scenarios:

  • What times can I get the company bus to commute to work?
  • How can I know if the bus is late?
  • What if the available bus stop is too far from my address?
